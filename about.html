Our Mission

GramaDharini is a free platform built to bridge the gap between rural students and opportunities. We believe every student — no matter where they are from — deserves access to internships, quality education, and career tools.

What We Offer

💼

Internships

Curated internship opportunities from companies across India, including remote options perfect for rural students.

📄

Resume Builder

Build a professional resume in minutes and download it as PDF — completely free, no registration needed.

📚

Free Courses

Access top free courses from YouTube, NPTEL, Google, and Coursera to build real skills.

🏆

Hackathons

Stay updated on upcoming hackathons, competitions, and events where you can win prizes and recognition.
